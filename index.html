Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions?

Everything parents and students ask before signing up.

Does my teen need coding experience?

Basic familiarity with any Python helps We teach Python fundamentals in Week 1. What matters most is curiosity and willingness to learn.

What ages / grades is this for?

High school students (grades 9–12), typically ages 14–18. The content is rigorous but accessible — we meet students where they are.

Is this live or pre-recorded?

100% live over Zoom. Every session is interactive with screen sharing, pair programming , and Q&A. Sessions are recorded in case you miss one.

What does my student walk away with?

A working AI agent (their own code on GitHub), a prompt engineering reference guide, and a portfolio project they can showcase on college applications.

How is this different from other courses?

Those programs cost 2x more and are taught by grad students. This course is taught by a working data scientist with 9+ years building production AI, in a cohort of 10, using cutting-edge tools like Claude Code.

Do you offer financial aid or payment plans?

Yes — we want motivated students regardless of budget. Contact us to discuss payment plans or need-based discounts.
